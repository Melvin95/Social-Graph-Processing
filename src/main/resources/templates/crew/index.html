About The Project

Using the Social Graph to quantify and analyze social media data

The Problem

Uber, although incredibly successful and dominating the digital ride-sharing industry, has been overwhelmed with controversy since its inception. The company has had no shortage of both internal and external issues. These issues have usually leaked onto mainstream media over and has occasionally caused outrage from the public.

Many people choose to express their outrage on Twitter. The popular social media network has had numerous ‘trends’ related to Uber controversy (http://fortune.com/2017/06/21/uber-controversy-timeline-travis-kalanick) as well as in South Africa (http://fortune.com/2017/04/24/uber-problems-africa-expansion). Despite a host of clone companies trying to take Uber’s spot, the company still holds the crown the the highest number of active users (Oct. 2017).
